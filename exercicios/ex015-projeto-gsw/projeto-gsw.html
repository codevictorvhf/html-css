Golden State Warriors, Congratulations!

Golden State Warriors, was crowned champion this weekend (Saturday, June 18). The team led by Steve Kerr faced the traditional and huge Boston Celtics franchise, in a best-of-7 game final.

There were 6 chilling games. All games had small advantages (biggest advantage was GSW in Game 5 in Boston, TD Garden). Where the Warriors team won by 121x112 - 9 points clear.
